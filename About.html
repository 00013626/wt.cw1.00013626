About us

Everbest Eucation is opened in Khazarasp district, Khorezm region in 2019 by the founder,Hurmatbek Akhmedov. At the moment there are a 4 teachers and a manager is working.Currently, There are about 200 students studying various courses. In Edu center, There are 5 types of courses available, Namely, Ielts, General English ,SAT and Math courses. All teachers are qualified and have at least 5 year experience.




We have about 400+ students who has achieved higher results and entered top ranked Universities. Everbest Edu is having its 7th year anniversary this year and recently, its first branch in tashkent. So, students who wanted to stydy in Everbest Edu but lives in Tashkent can go and study in Tahskent branch where they will have high quality lessons and they will make a path to their succesfull life.







What we do?

We are an Education center aimed to help students to enter their favourite and top ranked universitites and this is our top priority. We teach students from Ielts, SAT, General English and Math. Nowadays, We are aiming to add several types of courses, such as Korean,russian and chineese languages. And also we are planning to open IT Academy in Khazarasp which will be the first IT academy in Khazarasp.
